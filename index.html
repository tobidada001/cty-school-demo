Cty Group Of Schools

CTY is a Conventional school. It gives quality western and islamic education.The school has won many prizes and laurels in different competitions and the school has the record of being the 3rd best school in Ibadan.The school has standard classrooms for students, standard hostels for boarding students, well equipped libraries, well facilitated labouratories and computer rooms, staffrooms for male and female teachers, the examination room, the principal's office.
